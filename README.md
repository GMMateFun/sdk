## 📌 Overview

GMMate is a lightweight automation tool designed to simplify creator fee management on Pump.fun.

Instead of manually tracking and claiming rewards, GMMate handles everything for you:
- 🔍 Tracks your earnings in real-time  
- 💰 Claims your creator fees automatically  
- 📤 Sends funds directly to your wallet
